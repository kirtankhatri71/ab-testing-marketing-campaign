# 📊 A/B Testing — Marketing Campaign Analysis

## Business Problem
A marketing company wanted to know: do ads actually increase conversions compared to PSA campaigns?

## What I Did
- Analyzed 588,101 users across Ad and PSA groups
- Compared conversion rates by group, day, and hour
- Ran Chi-Square Test and Z-Test for statistical significance
- Built business recommendations based on findings

## Key Findings
- Ads converted at 2.55% vs PSA at 1.78% → **43% conversion lift**
- Difference is statistically significant (p < 0.05)
- Best days: Monday & Tuesday | Best time: 4 PM
- 95% Confidence Interval: [0.59%, 0.94%]
